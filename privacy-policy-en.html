Privacy Policy

We prioritize the privacy of your health and personal data.

1. Data Collection

We collect personal and medical data necessary to provide our services, including name, contact details, and medical history relevant to the requested tests.

2. Use of Information

Your data is used exclusively to conduct laboratory analyses, issue results, communicate regarding appointments, and improve our service quality.

3. Data Confidentiality

We adhere to the highest standards of medical confidentiality. Your results or data are not disclosed to any third party without your written consent, except for legally authorized official entities.

4. Data Protection

We implement advanced technical and administrative security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

5. Access to Results

You can obtain your results via our secure website, through SMS, or by receiving them in person from our branches after identity verification.

6. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze site traffic with anonymous data.

7. Updates

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy in compliance with new data protection regulations in the Kingdom.
