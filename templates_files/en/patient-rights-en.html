Patient Rights & Responsibilities

We are committed to providing services that preserve dignity and ensure the highest standards of care.

First: Patient Rights

  • Receiving high-quality laboratory medical care with full respect for human dignity.
  • Maintaining the confidentiality of all patient medical information in the lab.
  • Informing the patient of all details of laboratory procedures and potential risks, if any.
  • Obtaining a copy of analysis results and a full explanation of normal ranges.
  • Being in a safe and clean environment during sample collection.
  • The right to submit suggestions and complaints and receive a response from the lab management.

Second: Patient Responsibilities

  • Providing correct and accurate information about identity and medical history.
  • Complying with pre-examination instructions (such as fasting) to ensure result accuracy.
  • Respecting medical staff and employees and adhering to laboratory rules.
  • Adhering to booking times, especially for the home laboratory service.
  • Paying the required fees for medical services provided.
  • Informing the staff of any concerns or health reactions during sample collection.
